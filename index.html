Must-See Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is perhaps the most recognizable landmark in Paris. Standing at 324 meters tall, it offers breathtaking views of the city.

Open daily From €16

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris. It's home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.

Closed Tuesdays €17
